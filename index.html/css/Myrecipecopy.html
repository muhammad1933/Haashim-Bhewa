Chicken Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Take measure of 4 plates of cooked rice
  • 2 chicken filllets cut into small cubes
  • 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
  • 1 cup thinly sliced cabbage
  • 1/2 cup cooked shrimps(optional)
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn(optional)
  • 2 omlette thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon aginomoto(chinese salt)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoon crushed garlic paste crushed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 5 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • oil for stir frying

    • Instructions:

    1. In a bowl mix together your garlic paste,aginomoto,fish sauce,soy sauce,your water without forgetting your salt and pepper
    2. In a wok over high heat saute your cube chicken with 3 tablespoon of oil
    3. In the same wok in which you cooked your chicken,heat 2 tablespoon of oil over high heat and pour 2 plates of cooked rice
    4. Stir well to mix the rice with oil and the saute chicken for around 2 to 3 minutes
    5. Splash over your fried rice with around 3 tablespoon of the seasoning sauce you've prepared in the beginning and stir fry
    6. Then,mix your cooked chicken with your cooked shrimps as well as the vegetables and stir everything
    7. After that,top your omlette thinly sliced and put spring onions
    8. So, here is your fried rice chicken ready to serve and eat!
